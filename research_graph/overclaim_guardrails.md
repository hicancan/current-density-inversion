# Overclaim Guardrails

Do not claim:

1. Real QDM/NV validation is complete.
2. Real CAD/Gerber/GDS validation is complete.
3. PyPEEC is ground-truth real physics.
4. U-Net or topology-aware U-Net is a final reliable via detector.
5. No-via false positive is solved under real distributions.
6. Return-path diagnosis is solved under real distributions.
7. Few-shot generated-domain adaptation proves real-world adaptation.
8. Primary-label correctness means mechanism-level explanation.
9. DivB sanity alone solves inverse ambiguity.
10. PDN/KCL generation is automatically realistic without external validation.
11. A high accepted accuracy at low coverage is a full-coverage detector claim.
12. A hidden rejection rate is sufficient without accepted-hidden risk.
13. A lowest-residual model is automatically the correct physical hypothesis.
14. Synthetic multi-state improvement proves hardware-feasible active
    measurement.
15. Real-data interface scaffolding means real-data validation has been done.
