# Unlabeled Family Adaptation Proposal

Claim: `C14_unlabeled_family_adaptation`.

Proposed evidence loop:

1. Hold out a generated family.
2. Fit no labels on held-out rows.
3. Align evidence manifolds using high-confidence or distance-based signals.
4. Report accepted accuracy, accepted risk, reject rate, and family gap.
5. Stress the adapted model against hidden and near-hidden mechanisms.

Cannot claim: label-free adaptation works until this evidence loop is run.
