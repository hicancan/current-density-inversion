# Notes

## Why this experiment exists

Experiments 1 and 2 established the basic forward operator and single-layer observability.  Experiment 3 introduces the smallest multilayer setting that matters for the final route:

- two sheet layers;
- one vertical via;
- layer mixing;
- topology source/sink conservation;
- via detectability under sheet background.

## What counts as success

This experiment is successful if the ideal toy physics is internally consistent.  It is not required to solve the final inverse problem yet.

## What remains for later

- finite conductor width/thickness;
- return currents and ground planes;
- QDM sensor model;
- noisy inverse learning;
- CAD/FPC/EDA realism;
- COMSOL stress tests.
