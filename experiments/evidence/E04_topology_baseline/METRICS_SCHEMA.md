# exp04 Metrics Schema

## Baseline Metrics

Each method is reported under `benchmark.<split>.<method>`.

- `overall_rel_l2`: relative L2 over all five output channels.
- `truth_channel_l2_norm`: per-channel truth norm used to detect inactive channels.
- `active_channel_mask`: channels whose truth norm is large enough for relative L2 to be meaningful.
- `per_channel_rel_l2`: channel-wise relative L2 for active channels; inactive channels are reported as `null`.
- `per_channel_rmse_current_scale`: finite per-channel RMSE normalized by the maximum absolute truth current/source scale.
- `s1_rel_l2`: relative L2 for via/source channel.
- `via_loc_error_px_mean`: mean pixel localization error of max-|s1|.
- `via_hit_rate_within_2px`: fraction of samples whose via peak is within two pixels.
- `topology_mse`: MSE of `div J1 + s1` and `div J2 - s1` on the interior mask.
- `topology_l1_mean`: L1 topology residual.
- `layer_leakage_proxy`: predicted sheet energy in weak-target regions.
- `field_residual_proxy_rel_l2`: data-fitted linear forward-proxy residual against `B_clean`.
- `physical_reforward_rel_l2_to_bclean`: independent raster Biot-Savart
  re-forward residual against clean benchmark fields.
- `physical_reforward_rel_l2_to_bobs`: same residual against the observed input.
- `via_presence_precision/recall/f1`: via-presence metrics that remain defined
  for no-via samples.
- `via_false_positive_rate_no_via`: fraction of no-via samples whose predicted
  `s1` exceeds the presence threshold.

## Pareto And Stability Metrics

- `lambda_sweep`: short-run topology-lambda sweep. Each entry contains test and
  OOD metrics using the same schema as the benchmark methods.
- `lambda_sweep_summary.best_test_topology_lambda`: lambda with the lowest test
  topology MSE in the sweep.
- `lambda_sweep_summary.best_test_topology_ratio`: best sweep topology MSE
  divided by the sweep's lambda-zero topology MSE.
- `lambda_sweep_summary.best_test_l2_ratio`: overall L2 at the best topology
  lambda divided by lambda-zero overall L2.
- `multi_seed_summary.runs`: paired no-topology/topology short-run comparisons.
- `multi_seed_summary.topology_mse_ratio_mean/std`: mean and standard deviation
  of test topology MSE ratio across seeds.
- `multi_seed_summary.ood_topology_mse_ratio_mean/std`: mean and standard
  deviation of OOD topology MSE ratio across seeds.
- `stress_benchmark.<case>.<method>`: no-retraining evaluation on perturbed test
  inputs. Cases include noise, PSF blur, standoff-like gain tilt, channel
  mismatch, a combined perturbation, and `finite_width_return`.
- `stress_benchmark.<case>.stress_input.input_gap_rel_l2_to_clean`: relative L2
  difference between the perturbed/stress input and clean test field. For
  `finite_width_return`, this is the operator-mismatch gap generated by a
  finite-width/weak-return surrogate.
- `stress_summary.topology_mse_ratio_mean/max`: topology/no-topology ratios
  summarized across stress cases.
- `pypeec_operator_stress_bridge`: frozen read-only exp07 real PyPEEC artifact
  summary. It records exp07 backend status, case count, exp03-like shape gap,
  convergence delta, and per-case raw/shape gaps. It is not used for training,
  validation thresholds, or calibration.
- `pypeec_frozen_inference`: no-retraining inference on exp07's real-PyPEEC
  mini dataset. It reports the PyPEEC-vs-centerline input gap, the number of
  cases, explicit no-training/no-calibration flags, and method metrics for
  `unet_no_topology`, `unet_topology_soft_loss`, and
  `unet_topology_two_stage_refined`.
- `pypeec_frozen_inference.subsets`: subset diagnostics for canonical,
  exp03-like, via, no-via, dense-via, and return-path cases. These are
  descriptive frozen-test rows, not validation-selected thresholds.
- `pypeec_frozen_inference.null_via_diagnostics`: per-case no-via
  false-positive diagnostics on the frozen PyPEEC mini set. Rows include
  predicted `s1` peak/RMS/area, peak location, predicted via components,
  distance to trace/bend/return masks, topology residual, physical re-forward
  residual to PyPEEC, PyPEEC-centerline gap, leakage, residual detector score,
  and a failure-mode label. This table is diagnostic only and selects no
  PyPEEC-specific threshold.
- `pypeec_frozen_inference.null_via_diagnostics.mechanism_summary`:
  aggregation over false-positive no-via rows by mechanism. It reports counts,
  percentages, mean `s1` peak, topology residual, PyPEEC physical residual,
  PyPEEC-centerline gap, and distances to trace/bend/return masks.
- `pypeec_frozen_inference.null_via_diagnostics.failure_figure_paths`: selected
  representative false-positive figures under
  `outputs/figures/pypeec_null_via_failures/`.
- `pypeec_frozen_inference.return_path_diagnostics`: per-case return-current
  diagnostics on return-path PyPEEC cases. Rows include signal/return current
  energy fractions, layer-allocation error, signal/return path L2, excess via
  components, topology residual, leakage, raw PyPEEC physical re-forward
  residual, scalar-fitted PyPEEC shape residual, scalar fit, delta versus
  no-topology, and return-path failure/mechanism labels.
- `pypeec_frozen_inference.return_path_diagnostics.mechanism_summary`:
  aggregation over return-path rows by detailed mechanism. It separates
  amplitude mismatch, spatial-shape mismatch, layer-allocation mismatch, and
  bounded/mixed cases.
- `null_via_hypothesis_gate`: validation-only calibration artifact for the
  no-via hypothesis gate. It is selected on paired no-via/true-via synthetic
  validation stress families, reports before/after validation false-positive
  and recall metrics, stores selected score/artifact-zone parameters, and
  explicitly records that no PyPEEC case was used for threshold selection or
  calibration.
- `pypeec_frozen_inference.null_via_hypothesis_gate`: frozen PyPEEC
  before/after evaluation of the validation-selected gate. It reports all,
  no-via, via, dense-via, return-path, canonical, and exp03-like subset
  trade-offs for no-via false positives, via recall/F1, topology residual,
  and PyPEEC physical re-forward residual.
- `pypeec_frozen_inference.null_via_hypothesis_gate.pareto_rows`: descriptive
  frozen PyPEEC operating curve rows for validation-defined gate strengths.
  These rows report no-via FP, via recall/precision/F1, dense-via F1,
  return-path FP, topology MSE, and physical-B residual; they are not used to
  choose a PyPEEC threshold.
- `pypeec_frozen_inference.null_via_hypothesis_gate.case_figure_paths`:
  representative before/after figures for filtered false positives, rejected
  true-via cases, dense-via weakening, and return-path hard cases.
- `pypeec_frozen_inference.null_via_hypothesis_evidence`: frozen H1(true via)
  versus H0(artifact/no-via) evidence rows. These rows report evidence
  margins, uncertainty proxies, bend/return proximity, return-path evidence,
  and confidence/refusal labels. They are diagnostic only and do not select a
  PyPEEC threshold.
- `pypeec_frozen_inference.null_via_generative_hypothesis`: explicit frozen
  H1(with predicted `s1`) versus H0(same prediction with `s1=0`) re-forward
  energy comparison. Rows report `delta_evidence_h1_minus_h0`, H1/H0 energies,
  physical gain, artifact proximity, and a diagnostic decision. Summary rows
  include AUC, H1 precision/recall/F1, and no-via H1 false-positive rate.
- `pypeec_frozen_inference.null_via_generative_hypothesis.calibration_rows`:
  calibration-curve bins for `Delta evidence H1-H0` versus observed true-via
  rate. These are reported after frozen evaluation and are not used to select a
  PyPEEC threshold.
- `pypeec_frozen_inference.uncertainty_refusal`: summary of high-confidence via,
  probable artifact, and ambiguous/refusal categories derived from the H0/H1
  evidence rows. This is a reporting layer for identifiability, not a prediction
  rewrite.
- `pypeec_frozen_inference.selective_risk`: risk-coverage diagnostic for
  confidence-based refusal using absolute generative evidence margin. It reports
  coverage, selective risk/accuracy, selected-row via precision/recall, no-via
  false-positive rate within selected rows, and mean confidence.
- `pypeec_frozen_inference.heldout_split_protocol`: deterministic future
  PyPEEC calibration/held-out split definition. Current exp04 metrics keep all
  PyPEEC rows as frozen evaluation and set all current-use flags to false.
- `pypeec_frozen_inference.heldout_split_evaluation`: frozen metrics
  stratified by the reserved future calibration and held-out test roles. These
  role labels are not used for current calibration or threshold selection.
- `pypeec_frozen_inference.return_path_diagnostics.hypothesis_summary`:
  via-like versus return-current-like evidence classification for return-path
  cases.
- `pypeec_frozen_inference.return_current_aware_generator`: oracle diagnostic
  for an explicit signal-current plus scalar return-current basis on return-path
  cases. It uses known labels and reports whether that basis reduces PyPEEC
  return-path residual; it is not an inference model.
- `via_detection_benchmark.calibration_split`: split used to choose detector
  thresholds. This must remain `val`; test and OOD are evaluation-only.
- `via_detection_benchmark.calibration`: validation-split thresholds for raw
  adjoint, oracle sheet residual, U-Net sheet-residual via candidate detectors,
  and DoG/strict false-positive-controlled variants.
- `via_detection_benchmark.splits.<split>.<detector>`: detection-only via
  candidate metrics. These report localization, hit rate, precision/recall/F1,
  and no-via false-positive rate from score maps; they are not reconstructed
  current maps.
- `two_stage_refiner`: validation-calibrated residual-adjoint source scaling,
  threshold, blend, and DoG settings for the two-stage refined baseline.

## Gates

- U-Net no-topology must beat the zero baseline on test overall L2.
- U-Net topology-soft must reduce test topology MSE relative to U-Net no-topology.
- U-Net topology-soft must not degrade test overall L2 beyond the configured ratio.
- OOD topology MSE should improve relative to U-Net no-topology.
- Multi-seed mean topology MSE ratio must improve relative to no-topology.
- Lambda sweep must find a stronger topology-consistency point with bounded L2 cost.
- Robust current-scale channel metrics must be finite for test and OOD U-Net models.
- Stress-case mean topology MSE ratio must improve with bounded L2 cost.
- Physical re-forward residuals must be finite and the physics Tikhonov baseline
  must be reported.
- The topology-model residual via detector must beat raw-field adjoint detection
  on test localization and hit rate.
- The oracle residual via detector must remain a high-hit-rate upper bound.
- The DoG/strict topology residual detector must reduce OOD no-via false
  positives relative to the unfiltered topology residual detector while still
  beating raw OOD hit rate.
- The finite-width/return stress case must be reported with a measurable input
  gap.
- The finite-width/return stress case must report finite L2, topology, s1, via,
  leakage, and physical re-forward metrics for all U-Net methods.
- The real PyPEEC operator-stress bridge must report a valid exp07 artifact and
  a material but bounded exp03-like field-shape gap.
- The real PyPEEC frozen inference stress must report all three U-Net variants,
  finite metrics, explicit no-training/no-validation/no-calibration flags, and
  bounded topology/L2 ratios on the current mini dataset.
- The real PyPEEC frozen inference subset table must report canonical,
  exp03-like, via, no-via, dense-via, and return-path subsets.
- The real PyPEEC no-via false-positive diagnostics must report rows for every
  frozen U-Net variant and must explicitly remain no-threshold-selection
  failure analysis.
- The real PyPEEC return-path diagnostics must report rows for every frozen
  U-Net variant and must explicitly remain no-threshold-change failure
  analysis.
- The PyPEEC failure diagnostic core values must be finite.
- The PyPEEC mechanism summaries and representative no-via failure figures must
  be reported.
- The null-via hypothesis gate must be calibrated without PyPEEC threshold
  selection, must report finite synthetic validation metrics, and must report a
  frozen PyPEEC before/after trade-off table for all three U-Net variants.
- The null-via gate must also report a frozen Pareto curve and representative
  before/after case-study figures so FP reduction is not judged from a single
  operating point alone.
- The PyPEEC H0/H1 evidence comparison, uncertainty/refusal diagnostics,
  return-path hypothesis table, and future held-out split protocol must be
  present and must explicitly remain non-calibrating diagnostic/protocol
  artifacts.
- The PyPEEC generative H1/H0 scoring table must report explicit energy rows,
  calibration rows, and a calibration plot without selecting a PyPEEC threshold.
- The PyPEEC selective risk table must report risk-coverage rows and a plot.
- The PyPEEC return-current-aware generator must report finite return-path rows
  and must explicitly remain an oracle diagnostic, not model prediction.
- The PyPEEC held-out split evaluation must stratify current frozen metrics by
  the reserved future calibration/test roles without using those roles for
  calibration.
- The two-stage refined baseline must be present and keep test L2 within the
  configured bounded-cost gate.
